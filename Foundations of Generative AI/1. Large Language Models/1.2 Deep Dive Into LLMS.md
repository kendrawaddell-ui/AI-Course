# Deep Dive into Large Language Models 

To break down Andrej Karpathy's expert [3.5 hour video](https://youtu.be/7xTGNNLPyMI), complete the sequential activities as you watch and learn. 

## Tiktokenizer Tasks
1. Open [Tiktokenizer](https://tiktokenizer.vercel.app/) in a new window
2. Create four prompts, place each in the Tiktokenizer and notice the token count for each exmaple.
    - A short sentence (around 20-30 characters) using common English words
    - Add a mix of English and a high-cost language (e.g., Japanese, Greek, or Russian)
    - Add heavy punctuation/emojis (e.g., Hello!!! 😊🚀)
    - Add a very unusual, made-up word (e.g., Antidisestablishmentarianism or Zyzzyva)
3. What do you notice? What do you wonder?

## Neural Net Tasks
1. Open the [Transformer Neural Net 3D visualizer](https://bbycroft.net/llm) in a new window
2. Look to the Chapter Overview panel on the left. Click through the blue Continue button and follow along in the visualizer for as long as your brain can stay afloat. Deep learning of this content is not required; this is a cursory exploration.
3. Compare the different GPT models.
4. What do you notice? What do you wonder?

## LMArena Tasks
1. Open [LMArena](https://arena.ai/) in a new window.
2. Notice the three ways to explore models: Battle, Side-by-Side, and Direct Chat. Write a prompt in each of these methods and observe the outputs.
3. Notice the different types of inputs accepted: text prompts, web search, image generation, and apps/websites. Write a prompt using each of these inputs and observe the outputs.
4. Click on the leaderboard and explore it.
5. What do you notice? What do you wonder?
